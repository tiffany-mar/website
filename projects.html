Survive The Forest

Augest 2018-September 2018, Computer Science Principles

This purpose of this project was to create a game or storyline with a great amount of user interaction. My partner and I decided to make a storyline in which the user must try to survive in a forest. The user are faced with different scenarios and they are given choices to make. We worked side by side and discussed every part to make sure it included both of our ideas. In this project, I cleaned up animations and programmed new scenes. We did encounter some challenges with the animations messing up. We were able to pinpoint the problem and find solutions. There was an instance where we wanted a "sparks flying" effect, but it would die out after a mere couple of seconds. I was able to figure out that there was a limit to the amount of clones you could have, so I would kill a clone after its job was done.

Oh My Spikes

September 2018-October 2018, Computer Science Principles

The purpose of this project was to create an app. My partner and I decided to make a game. In our game, a user controls the vertical movement of a bird which consistently moves along the x-axis of the screen. The goal is to reach the edge of a screen without crashing into spikes. We split up how much and what parts we would program. It was my role to organize the documentation. I helped make the spikes move and set new y-positions for the spikes. My partner and I were having trouble making our spikes move. We both proposed many different ways of solving the problem and tested many variations of code. We finally found a way in which make the spike "head" in a certain direction and add spikes to all sides of the block.
